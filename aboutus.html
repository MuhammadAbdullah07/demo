Why are we here?

RUDN Enclave, a real estate development project by RMRSCO (PVT. Ltd), is a remarkable venture situated at a prime location on Main Adyala Road, Ring Road Rawalpindi. The renowned NESPAK has been appointed as the Master Planner & Consultant for the project. Additionally, the development team, including Urban Planning and Design Limited (UPDL) and its subsidiaries, Alhaq builders and New Leafs, has been incorporated as master developers and contractors. RUDN Enclave is an eco-friendly and healthy housing society, boasting a scenic view of the Dam and beautiful natural surroundings, as well as easy access to the motorway (Chakri & Thalian Interchange) and the new International Islamabad Airport. All the necessary amenities are readily available, making it a unique project in the area. It is worth noting that bookings were opened once 25% of the development work was already completed on-site. The housing society offers residential plots, commercial plots, farmhouses, high-rise buildings, business centers, and cinemas.
